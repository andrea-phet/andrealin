Projectile Motion

Projectile Motion is a free online educational computer program that allows students to interactively explore the physics of 2D motion. In fall of 2017, I published Projectile Motion with PhET, a project based in University of Colorado Boulder to create free interactive math and science simulations. The simulation has been translated by volunteers around the world into over 25 languages and used by students and teachers close to a million times.
